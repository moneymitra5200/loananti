## Project Overview

This lending management system includes:

### Core Modules
1. **Loan Management** - Application, approval, disbursement
2. **Accounting** - Trial Balance, P&L, Daybook, Ledger
3. **Multi-Company** - Support for multiple lending companies
4. **EMI Management** - Payment tracking and collection

### Accounting Features
- Bank Balance tracking
- Cashbook management
- Equity management
- Borrowed Money tracking
- Investment tracking
- Loan Principal & Interest Receivable

### Trial Balance Structure
**LEFT SIDE (Liabilities):**
- Equity (with Add button)
- Borrowed Money (with Add button)
- Final Equity (calculated)

**RIGHT SIDE (Assets):**
- Bank Balance (auto)
- Cashbook Balance (auto)
- Invest Money (with Add button)
- Loan Principal (auto)
- Interest Receivable (pending interest)
